About LORENZO Electrical

Our story, mission, and commitment to excellence

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by LORENZO, our company began as a small electrical service provider in Johannesburg. Through hard work, dedication to quality, and exceptional customer service, we've grown to serve clients across South Africa.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and professional electrical services that exceed our clients' expectations while maintaining the highest standards of workmanship and integrity.

Our Team

Our team consists of certified electricians with extensive training and experience. We invest in continuous education to stay current with the latest electrical codes, technologies, and safety standards.

Safety First

Safety is our top priority. All our work complies with South African electrical regulations, and we carry comprehensive insurance for your peace of mind.

Our Core Values

Safety

We prioritize safety in every project, ensuring protection for both our team and your property.

Quality

We use only the highest quality materials and adhere to strict workmanship standards.

Reliability

We show up on time and complete projects as promised, with minimal disruption.

Integrity

Honest communication and fair pricing are the foundations of our business.
